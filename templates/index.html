A computer is a programmable device that stores, retrieves, and processes data. The term "computer" was originally given to
humans (human computers) who performed numerical calculations using mechanical calculators, such as the abacus and
slide rule. The term was later given to a mechanical device as they began replacing the human computers. Today's computers
are electronic devices that accept data (input), process that data, produce output, and store (storage) the results.
